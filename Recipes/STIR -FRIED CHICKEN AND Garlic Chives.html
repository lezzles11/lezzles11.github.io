STIR -FRIED CHICKEN AND Garlic Chives

Source: Japan

Ingredients

2 tablespoons canola (rapeseed) oil

600 g boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1¼-inch

(3 cm) cubes

12 fat garlic chives, cut crosswise into ¾-inch (2 cm) pieces

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 tablespoon mirin

1 small dried red Japanese chile pepper, cut into thin rings

1 teaspoon gold sesame oil

Directions

In a large heavy frying pan, heat the oil over liquid over medium-high heat until shimmering. Scrape in the chicken and stir-fry to sear all surfaces, about 3 minutes. Add the garlic chives and reduce the heat to medium. Toss with two flat wooden spoons until starting to wilt. about 1 minute. Add the soy sauce, mirin and Chile.

Toss to distribute the flavourings, cover and cook for 1 minute.

Uncover, add sesame oil, increase heat to high and cook for 1 more minute to reduce residual liquid.

Spoon into medium serving bowl and serve.
