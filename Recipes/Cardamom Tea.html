Cardamom Tea

★★★★

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 3-4 Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

450 ml/16 fl oz/scant 2 cups water

20 ml/4 tsp leaf tea, preferably Darjeeling

5-6 green cardamom pods, bruised

300 ml/10 fl oz/1¼ cups milk

sugar, to taste

Directions

Put the water, tea and cardamom pods into a pan and bring to the boil.

Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 6-8 minutes.

Add the milk to the pan and increase the heat slightly. Simmer, uncovered, for 5-6 minutes, until the tea has turned a pinkish-brown colour.

Add more milk to taste. Strain into cups, add sugar to taste and serve.

Nutrition

Per portion Energy 42kcal/177kJ; Protein 2.9g; Carbohydrate 4.6g, of which sugars 3.8g; Fat 1.6g, of which saturates 0.8g; Cholesterol 4mg; Calcium 95mg; Fibre 0g; Sodium 42mg.
