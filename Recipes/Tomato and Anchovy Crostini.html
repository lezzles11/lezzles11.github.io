Tomato and Anchovy Crostini

Source: Quick and Easy Italian Recipes

Ingredients

4 slices Tuscan or other rustic bread

4 canned anchovy fillets, drained

and cliced

2 tomatoes, sliced

1 pearl (baby) onion, chopped

1 tablespoon chopped basil

extra virgin olive oil, for drizzling

Directions

Preheat the oven to 350°F/180°C/Gas Mark 4 and preheat the broiler (grill) to medium-high heat. Toast the slices of bread under the broiler for 1-2 minutes on each side, or until golden.

Divide the anchovy, slices of tomato, a little chopped onion, and basil between each slice of toast. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt. Put the toasts on a baking sheet and heat in the oven for 4-5 minutes, then serve.
