Ginger Tea

Difficulty: Easy Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

1 cup water

1 teaspoon lemon juice, or to taste

Â½ to 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger

sugar or honey to taste

Directions

Boil the water and pour into a mug or cup. Add the lemon juice. Place a small strainer on the cup so that its bottom is submerged. Add the minced fresh ginger to the strainer and let steep for several minutes.

Remove the strainer, add sugar or honey to taste, and enjoy.
