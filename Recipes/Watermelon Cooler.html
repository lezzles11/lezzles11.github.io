Watermelon Cooler

★★★★

English

Servings: 6 Source: Gordon Ramsay

Ingredients

2 pounds seeded watermelon flesh, cut into chunks

Juice of 2 limes

Pinch of salt

Directions

Put the watermelon chunks into a blender and blitz until smooth. Add the lime juice and a pinch of salt and blend again.

Add 2 handfuls of ice and blend once more or until the ice is broken up. If the mixture is very thick, blend in ½ cup of cold water.

Serve in glasses with extra ice cubes or pour into your water bottle to take with you.
